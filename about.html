About

It was November 27, 2017 that the phrase “I Find Myself Here” was coined by myself, David Ebanks. It just happened! I was doing a reel for instagram, I said it, and never looked back. On November 11, 2021, I made IFMH into a vlog then changed all my social media handles to IFMH. It wasn’t until February 18, 2023 though when I got a heat pressed shirt with the IFMH youtube banner from my parents as a birthday gift, that I decided to step things up. In June I printed my first 22 shirts, and in September my order of 250 blanks had arrived. This clothing brand will be the next Nike. The world will be a better place as we spread the word that today, in the here and now, is an opportunity to find ourselves being generous, grateful and gracious which is a good harvest we can enjoy daily.
